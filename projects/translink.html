01
UX Design Case Study

Interactive
Zone Map

Redesigning the TransLink SkyTrain fare map to simplify the zone-based system for new and infrequent riders.

Year: Spring 2025

Built for: IAT 333 - Interaction Design Methods

Role: Lead UX Researcher & Designer

objective

The existing TransLink SkyTrain fare system presents a significant cognitive load for infrequent riders. The "Fare Zone" logic creates high friction at the point of purchase.

→

outcome

By visualizing the journey before payment, our solution reduced the average decision time by 40% in user testing sessions.

my roles

01.

UX Research
Led cued-recall debriefs and field studies at Waterfront Station.

02.

Video Lead
Directed the user journey video to bridge wireframes and physical reality.

03.

Collaboration
Facilitated workshops to align design decisions with stakeholder goals.
